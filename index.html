Our Process

At Spot On Digital, we ensure a smooth and efficient process to bring your vision to life. Here’s how it works:

1. Consultation & Briefing

Share Your Vision: Tell us about your project, goals, and design requirements.
Project Discussion: We analyze your needs and suggest the best creative solutions.

2. Quotation & Approval

Receive a Quote: Based on your requirements, we provide a detailed cost estimate.
Approval & Agreement: Once agreed, we finalize timelines and expectations.
Deposit to Get Started: 50% deposite is required to begin the project.

3. Research & Concept Development

Market & Brand Analysis: We research your industry, competitors, and target audience.
Concept Creation: Our team brainstorms and develops initial design ideas tailored to your brand.

4. Design & Revisions

First Drafts:We present the initial designs for your feedback.
Revisions & Refinements: We make adjustments based on your input to perfect the design.

5. Finalization & Delivery

Approval & Final Touches: Once you're satisfied, we finalize the designs.
Full Payment: The remaining balance is due upon delivery of the final files.
Files Delivered:You receive the high-quality design files in the required formats.

6. Ongoing Support (If Needed)

Additional Services: Need branding, printing, or digital marketing? We’ve got you covered!
After-Service Support: We’re always here to assist you with future design needs.
